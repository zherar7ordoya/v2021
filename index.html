Portada

No olvidar de escribir el contexto de cada contexto. In other words, don’t just showcase the images, or the names of what you did, or the companies you worked for. Instead, focus on how you can deliver value. Talk about the goals that have been reached and how your involvement made that possible. This provides a lot of context and tells the person reading why the project was a success.
